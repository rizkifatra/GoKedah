Mount Jerai

Mount Jerai, formerly Kedah Peak, is a mountain in Kedah, Malaysia with a height of 3,993 feet. It is located at the border of Kuala Muda and Yan districts. Known as the Hausberg of Sungai Petani, the capital of Kuala Muda.
